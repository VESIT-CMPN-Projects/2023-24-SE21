Mission Statement

Our mission at Nishulkh Bhojan Seva is to eliminate food waste and foster food donation at the grassroots level. We strive towards connecting NGOs and Food partners to alleviate food insecurity. Here, we enable access to food by redistribution, volunteering and providing connectivity.Join us to combat food waste and work towards UN Sustainable Development Goal 2.
